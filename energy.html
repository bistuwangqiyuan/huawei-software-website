Our AI+Energy Solutions

Smart Inspection

Our Smart Inspection System combines AI technology with IoT devices to revolutionize energy infrastructure inspection processes. By leveraging computer vision, machine learning, and data analytics, we provide comprehensive monitoring and predictive maintenance for power transmission lines, substations, and power distribution networks.

  • Automated fault detection and classification with 98% accuracy
  • Thermal imaging analysis for equipment health monitoring
  • Drone-based inspection capabilities for hard-to-reach areas
  • Real-time monitoring and alert system for critical infrastructure
  • Predictive maintenance scheduling to prevent equipment failures
  • Integrated reporting and analytics dashboard

Smart Monitoring

Our Smart Monitoring System provides comprehensive safety monitoring and management for energy facilities. Using advanced AI algorithms, IoT sensors, and big data analytics, we help energy companies ensure operational safety, reduce risks, and improve emergency response capabilities.

  • Real-time personnel tracking and management in hazardous environments
  • Intelligent video surveillance with behavior analysis
  • Environmental parameter monitoring (gas levels, temperature, humidity)
  • Automated safety violation detection and warning
  • Emergency response coordination system
  • Comprehensive safety performance analytics and reporting
