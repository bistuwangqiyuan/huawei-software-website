Our AI+Water Resources Solutions

Smart Water Conservancy

Our Smart Water Conservancy solution integrates AI technology with traditional water conservancy infrastructure to create intelligent management systems for dams, reservoirs, and flood control. By leveraging IoT sensors, computer vision, and big data analytics, we help water resource authorities optimize operations, improve safety, and enhance decision-making capabilities.

  • Real-time monitoring of water levels, flow rates, and infrastructure integrity
  • AI-powered flood prediction and early warning systems
  • Automated gate control and optimization for dams and sluices
  • Digital twin technology for scenario simulation and planning
  • Advanced analytics for long-term water resource planning
  • Mobile applications for field staff and management

Smart Water Affairs

Our Smart Water Affairs solution addresses the complex challenges of urban water management, supply, and quality monitoring. We provide comprehensive digital platforms that connect water treatment facilities, distribution networks, and end-user consumption patterns to create a fully integrated water management ecosystem.

  • Intelligent water quality monitoring and contamination detection
  • Leak detection and pipeline integrity assessment systems
  • Smart metering infrastructure and consumption analytics
  • Water treatment process optimization using AI algorithms
  • Demand forecasting and supply optimization
  • Integrated command center for unified water affairs management

Smart Water Resources

Our Smart Water Resources solution provides advanced tools for comprehensive water resource planning, allocation, and protection. By utilizing satellite imagery, AI analytics, and hydrological modeling, we help authorities make informed decisions about water resource utilization while ensuring ecological balance and sustainability.

  • Basin-wide water resource monitoring and assessment
  • AI-driven resource allocation optimization
  • Groundwater level and quality monitoring
  • Watershed health assessment and ecological flow management
  • Climate change impact analysis on water resources
  • Water rights management and allocation systems

Smart Water Ecology

Our Smart Water Ecology solution focuses on maintaining and restoring healthy aquatic ecosystems. Through continuous monitoring, AI-based species identification, and habitat assessment, we provide comprehensive tools for ecological protection and restoration of rivers, lakes, and wetlands.

  • AI-powered aquatic species identification and tracking
  • Real-time water quality parameter monitoring for ecological health
  • Habitat assessment and restoration planning tools
  • Automated algal bloom detection and early warning
  • Ecological flow management recommendations
  • Environmental impact assessment and ecological compensation
