Company Profile

Company always takes it as its own responsibility to create high-precision products that serve the national strategy and meet the market demand. In order to control the development trend of information technology, it has established the Information Technology Innovation Research Institute (For Information Innovation, Fiberhome (ForInN) Research Institute), and established a "Data Governance and Access Optimization" joint laboratory with Beijing Information Science and Technology University.

luisuantech Software is one of the earliest AI+ companies engaged in on-site intelligent software and hardware research and development and services in China. After more than 20 years of development, it has become a supplier of on-site intelligent operation products and overall solutions.

Adhering to the concept of "AI Empowerment, Creating a Better Tomorrow Together", luisuantech Software has developed into an artificial intelligence solution provider for scenarios such as AI+Energy, AI+Transportation, AI+Water Resources, and AI+Agriculture.
